#2 Eating in Sha Tin

As a resident of Sha Tin, getting to know the surrounding restaurants will help to increase the enjoyment of eating. After scrapping data, cleaning data and building a database, I screened and sorted the information of restaurants around Sha Tin.
The two charts show the ten most highly rated Cantonese restaurants in Sha Tin and the ten cuisines with most restaurants in Sha Tin, together with the average of the positive and negative comments for reference. The number of Japanese, Western, Hong Kong-style and Cantonese restaurants is the largest.
